Healing Through Music

Our music therapy programming has demonstrated efficacy in reducing symptoms of PTSD, anxiety, and depression—common challenges faced by survivors—by fostering emotional regulation, safety, and nonverbal trauma processing, offering a path to healing for survivors.

GROUP MUSIC THERAPY

Wings of Resilience

Our core year-round program, grounded in proven positive outcomes, cultivates a supportive community through secure, intimate workshops. These sessions foster healing and build essential connections among survivors navigating crises.

Learn More
ALUMNI MUSIC THERAPY

Jam Sessions

Survivors who have completed a music therapy workshop with EchoKind, our alumni, are invited to join ongoing monthly support groups facilitated by our board-certified music therapists.

Learn More
CHILDREN'S PROGRAM

Brave Beats

When fleeing abuse, mothers and their children often seek refuge in shelters. We bring joy and foster positive coping skills through music therapy, adapted for each age group.

Learn More
THERAPEUTIC SONGWRITING

Songs of Survival

This deeply personal music therapy program employs songwriting as a therapeutic modality. Working closely with a board-certified music therapist, clients write original songs that express their individual experiences. The songs are professionally recorded and made available for purchase or download.

Learn More
ALUMNI MUSIC THERAPY

Virtual Choir

Our Virtual Choir unites survivors, advocates, and musicians nationwide through a powerful video and music experience. Together, they learn and perform a song, fostering solidarity and amplifying survivor voices.
